Common Questions

Curious? We have answers.

Does Botox hurt? +
Most clients describe it as a tiny pinch. The needles are ultra-fine and treatment takes only minutes. Numbing cream is available if desired.
When will I see results? +
You will start noticing changes in 3–5 days, with full effect at 14 days. Results typically last 3–6 months.
Will I look frozen? +
Not with us. Our approach emphasizes natural movement. We use precise dosing to soften lines while preserving your expressions.
Is it safe? +
Absolutely. Botox has been FDA-approved for cosmetic use since 2002 with a strong safety record when administered by trained professionals.
What is the downtime? +
Zero downtime. You may have small red bumps for 15–30 minutes. Avoid lying down or exercising for 4 hours post-treatment.
How do I prepare? +
Avoid alcohol, aspirin, and ibuprofen for 24 hours before to minimize bruising. Come with a clean face if possible.
